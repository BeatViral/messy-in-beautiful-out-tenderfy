# Generate a Messy In → Beautiful Out company demo

Use this repository as the reusable baseline. Do not rebuild the experience from scratch unless the company brief requires a material interaction change.

## Inputs

```text
COMPANY_BRIEF={{COMPANY_BRIEF}}
TARGET_REPOSITORY={{TARGET_REPOSITORY}}
COMPANY_URL={{COMPANY_URL}} (optional supporting source)
```

## Required approach

1. Establish what the company publicly offers today from the brief and supplied sources.
2. Identify a credible adjacent opportunity involving valuable operational knowledge not yet captured in the normal content or workflow.
3. Keep the central transformation: a person tells a work story; the product separates documented evidence, human contribution, and verification required; it prepares structured material for the appropriate reviewer.
4. Retain the concise phone-to-output demo and short selectable stories. Tailor personas, evidence types, terminology, and requirements to the company.
5. Clearly label the page as an independent concept opportunity. Do not imply live integration, website analysis, automated verification, or a production feature.
6. Update the target repository metadata and GitHub Pages configuration, run the build, then commit and push intentionally.

If there is no evidence for a genuine opportunity beyond the existing product, explain the limitation rather than inventing product claims.
